Our Mission at KR Redeemed

We here at KR Redeemed Rebuilds beleive it is our mission to put trust back into the automobile repair business. Everyone knows that if you find a good mechanic, you had better hold on to him because they are worth their weight in gold. Unless you have a reasonable knowledge of cars and automobile repair you are at the mercy of the mechanic repairing your absolutely necessary vehicle (in today's vehicle-centric world).

Kevin beleieves it is our mission in life to help others. We believe, with our love of cars, especially Chevy S10 shortbed pickups, we here at KR Redeemed Rebuilds can be most useful by helping others with their automobile problems or interest.
